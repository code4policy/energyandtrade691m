Other Renewables Energy Trend

The graph illustrates energy consumption per capita from "Other Renewables" (measured in kWh-equivalent) from 1990 to 2023. Germany and the United Kingdom lead in adopting other renewable energy sources, showcasing a significant upward trajectory, reflecting their policy commitments to diversify renewable energy portfolios.

The United States maintains steady per capita consumption, reflecting its consistent use of diverse renewable sources. Meanwhile, countries like the Netherlands, South Korea, and Japan show gradual increases in their reliance on other renewables, demonstrating ongoing efforts to expand sustainable energy usage.

China, although starting from lower levels, has shown consistent growth in recent years, aligning with its broader renewable energy strategies. This trend underscores the importance of integrating diverse renewable energy sources into global energy systems to reduce carbon emissions and support sustainable growth.
