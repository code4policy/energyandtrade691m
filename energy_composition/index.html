About U.S. Energy Production

The United States produces energy from various sources, including renewable energy like solar and wind, as well as non-renewable sources such as coal, natural gas, and nuclear power. This chart displays the trends and composition of U.S. energy production over the years, reflecting the shift towards more sustainable practices.

Understanding these trends helps policymakers and industry leaders make informed decisions that balance energy demands with environmental sustainability.
